Sea

The Blue Sea is a huge body of water spanning across the entire world Nearly everything in the world can be found on the Sea save for sky islands such as Skypiea and Weatheria.

the Blue Sea used to be around 200 meters lower than its current levels but the events of the Void Century caused it to rise by that much sinking many continents in the process Dr. Vegapunk predicts that the Blue Sea is set to engulf the rest of the world's landmasses in the future

The main governmental body of the Blue Sea is the World Government which currently exercises control over a hundred-seventy countries. The Revolutionary Army is the main force opposing the World Government and they control multiple islands including Baltigo and Momoiro Island.

The rise of the sea caused worldwide destruction and the death of millions forcing survivors to seek mountaintops and higher grounds in order to establish new communities which led to the formation of many of the world's islands in the present time.

It is theorized that those with Devil Fruit powers exist in an "unnatural state" and the sea being the mother of nature detests them for it thus taking away their ability to swim as punishme
